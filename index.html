About Garibnath Tutorials

Building Strong Foundations for Classes 4–12

Garibnath Tutorials (GT) is a learner-first institute in Ahiyapur, Muzaffarpur, focused on concept clarity, disciplined practice, and personal mentorship. Led by Managing Director Sachin Kumar Chaudhary & Director Mr Abhishek Kumar, our team helps students excel in Science, Maths, English & SST through structured lessons, smart assessments, and doubt-clearing support.
